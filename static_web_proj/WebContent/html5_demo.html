Article 1 header

Development Lead and Software Architect for ESP Studio IDE, which is bundled with the Sybase Event Streaming Platform (ESP). After the acquisition of Aleri by Sybase I assumed the role of Development Lead on ESP Studio. ESP Studio was targeted by management and marketing as 1 of the top 3 components deemed critical to the success of the Sybase CEP platform. During the course of this project I was responsible for the overall architecture of ESP studio as well as managing the virtual ESP Studio team which was comprised of 4 software engineers. Sybase adopted the SAP agile software process shortly after the project began in the spring of 2010. Development Lead of Studio virtual team consisting of 4 development engineers Architecture of ESP Studio Design, coding and debugging of visual and textual authoring of CCL. Usability of ESP Studio and associated platform components. Work with QA team to build automated regression suites for the Studio user interface. Construct junit tests for various parts of the Studio UI. ESP Studio project schedule construction Presentation of Studio architecture and Studio scrum demos as needed to management and team. Assist technical writers to document the Studio components in the user manual
